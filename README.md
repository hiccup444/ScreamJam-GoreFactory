### _Where am I?_

I only remember being _taken_. My boss, my co-workers and I...we were...we went out for drinks after work and...then, a van grabbed us as we were walking. Their faces were covered. There was malice behind their intent. Only words I heard were, "More bodies..." and "The Director is going to be happy with [these ones]. They look **_healthy_**..."

我只记得被抓走了。我的老板，同事和我......我们......我们下班以后出门喝酒，然后......我们走路着被货车抓住了。他们的脸都掩藏的。他们的意图满怀恶意。我唯一听到的话就是 “身体更多.....” 然后 “主任会喜欢这些。看起来非常健康......”

We were brought to some _warehouse?_ I could only hear and _smell_ things as we were separated...I don't remember anything else. What is that sound? _An electric saw?_

我们被带去某一个......仓库？我们被分开的时候，我只听见又闻某些东西和味道......其他的事我都记不清楚。那个声音是什么？一条电锯吗？
